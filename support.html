Getting Started

  • Browse the Exercises tab to see 60+ pre-loaded exercises or add your own
  • Create a training cycle from the Cycles tab — set a name, length (1–10 weeks), and toggle auto-progression
  • Add sessions (workout days) and assign exercises to each one
  • Publish your cycle and start your first workout from the Sessions tab

Auto-Progression

When auto-regulate is enabled, Ant uses your logged Reps In Reserve (RIR) and post-workout feedback to recommend weight and rep adjustments for your next session. Each exercise and muscle group is tracked independently.

You can turn auto-progression off at any time in your cycle settings to train with straight sets.

iCloud Sync

Ant syncs your data across all your Apple devices automatically via iCloud. Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device settings. If you experience sync delays, try opening the app on both devices and waiting a few moments.

Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account — it is never accessible to us or any third party.

Body Measurements

Track weight, body fat, chest, waist, hips, arms, and more from the Body tab. Switch between metric and imperial units anytime in Settings — all values convert instantly.

Data Management

Export your exercise library as a JSON file from Settings to share with friends or back up your custom exercises. Import exercises from a shared file the same way.

To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, remove synced data from Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ant includes a comprehensive FAQ inside the app. Open any tab, tap the gear icon to open Settings, then tap FAQ for detailed answers on cycles, sessions, exercises, RIR, deloads, and more.
