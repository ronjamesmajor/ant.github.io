Getting Started

  • Subscribe or start a free trial, then sign in with Apple to create your account
  • For a quick workout, tap Quick Session on the Sessions tab, add exercises, and start
  • For a structured program, create a training cycle from the Cycles tab or browse built-in templates
  • Add sessions (workout days), assign exercises, publish, and start from the Sessions tab
  • Browse the Exercises tab to see 90+ pre-loaded exercises or add your own

Auto-Progression

When auto-regulate is enabled, Ant uses your logged Reps In Reserve (RIR) and post-workout feedback to recommend weight and rep adjustments for your next session. Each exercise and muscle group is tracked independently.

You can turn auto-progression off at any time in your cycle settings to train with straight sets.

Sync & Account

Ant syncs your data securely via Sign in with Apple. Your workouts are saved locally for offline use and pushed to a secure server automatically when connected. Sign in on a new device to download your full history.

Your data is protected by row-level security. No other user or third party can access your workouts.

Body Measurements

Track weight, body fat, chest, waist, hips, arms, and more from the Body tab. Switch between metric and imperial units anytime in Settings — all values convert instantly.

Data Management

Export your exercise library as a JSON file from Settings to share with friends or back up your custom exercises. Import exercises from a shared file the same way.

To reset local data, use Reset Local Cache in Settings — this re-downloads everything from the server. To delete your account and all data, contact ant-workout@pm.me.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ant includes a comprehensive FAQ inside the app. Open any tab, tap the gear icon to open Settings, then tap FAQ for detailed answers on cycles, sessions, exercises, RIR, deloads, and more.
