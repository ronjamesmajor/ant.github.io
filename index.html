Privacy Policy Support

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

Ant does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App does not use analytics, advertising frameworks, or third-party SDKs.

Data Storage

All data you enter — workouts, exercises, body measurements, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud sync through your device settings, your data may sync across your Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. This is handled entirely by Apple's CloudKit infrastructure and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Ant does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms. The App is built exclusively with Apple-native frameworks.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the App from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also remove synced data through your iCloud storage settings.

Children's Privacy

Ant does not knowingly collect data from children. The App does not collect data from anyone.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact ant-workout@pm.me

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
