Data Collection

Ant does not sell, share, or monetize your personal data. The App does not use analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking SDKs. The only data transmitted is your workout data, synced to a secure server so you can access it across devices.

Authentication

Ant requires Sign in with Apple to create your account. Apple provides a pseudonymous user identifier to the App. Ant does not receive or store your name, email address, or Apple ID password. Your identity is protected by Apple's privacy infrastructure.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places: locally on your device (for fast, offline access) and on a secure cloud server powered by Supabase (the source of truth for sync). All data is transmitted over HTTPS and access is protected by row-level security tied to your authenticated account. Your data is never accessible to other users.

Third-Party Services

Ant uses Supabase for secure cloud data storage and authentication. Supabase is an open-source backend platform hosted on AWS infrastructure. No other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising SDKs are used. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Data Deletion

You can delete all local data at any time by deleting the App from your device. To delete your server-side data, use the "Reset Local Cache" option in Settings, or contact ant-workout@pm.me to request full account deletion.

Children's Privacy

Ant is intended for users 18 years of age or older. The App does not knowingly collect data from children.

Medical Disclaimer

Ant is a workout tracking and progression tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The exercise recommendations generated by the app are based on your logged performance data and general progressive overload principles — they are not a substitute for professional guidance.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting, modifying, or intensifying any exercise program, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions, injuries, or concerns. Stop exercising immediately and seek medical attention if you experience pain, dizziness, or discomfort.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact ant-workout@pm.me
